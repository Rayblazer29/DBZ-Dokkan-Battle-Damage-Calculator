Evidence

The formula for calculation of damage is as follows:


Damage = (EnemyAttackStat * (SuperAttackMultiplier - AttackLoweringEffectFromSuper) * (100% - AttackLoweringPassive) * (100% - TotalDamageReduction) * TypeEffectiveness * variance - DefenseStat) * GuardMultiplier

Note: variance is fixed at 1.03, as damage ranges from 1x to 1.03x of original value
